STAKEHOLDER ANALYSIS

"Music creators: African-based musicians, artists, and creators who will be featured on the platform and will benefit from the exposure and revenue generated by the service."

Live@ can engage with music creators by offering them a fair and transparent process for selecting and compensating performers, and by providing them with tools and resources to promote their music and engage with their fans.

Live@ can engage with subscribers by providing them with a high-quality, diverse range of live music, performances, and virtual reality experiences, and by listening to their feedback and addressing any concerns they may have. Live@ can engage with investors by providing them with regular updates on the company's performance, financials, and growth plans, and by being transparent about the risks and rewards of investing in the company.

Live@ can engage with media by providing them with access to the service, interviews with key executives, and relevant data and research.
